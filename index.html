About Me

Published: Mon 01 May 2023
By Gioacchino Castorio

In About Me.

My name is Gioacchino Castorio. but people call me Jack or Kino. By day I troubleshoot dot11 wireless network, by night I like to call myself MI0YTF as an amateur radio operator.

I spend most of my time working out, going for walks, reading technical books, or writing my own video games and utility programs in Python.

I created this blog to share tips and tricks when troubleshooting, avoiding some common pitfalls, while heavily relying on network programmability and automation which have greatly simplified my day-to-day.
